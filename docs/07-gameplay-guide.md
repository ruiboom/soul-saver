# SOUL SAVER — Gameplay Guide

*How to survive Hell, arm yourself with relics, and bring Lucia home.*

---

## 1. The goal

You are **Father Anselm**, alive and in a state of grace, walking through the Ashen
Reaches of Hell. Everything here wants to extinguish you, forever, in growing numbers.

A run ends one of three ways:

1. **THE GATE OPENS** *(true ending)* — recover all **seven Vestiges of Lucia's soul**
   from the seven Profane Shrines, then reach the **Gate of Dawn** at the map's centre
   once it opens during the final battle.
2. **DAWN, WITHOUT HER** *(survivor ending)* — outlast the night: survive the Warden of
   the Gate (kill it, or stay alive until the final bell at 21:00) without the full seven.
3. **HELL KEEPS HIM** — your Consecration hits zero. Everything you banked
   (Ossuary Marks, Vestige Book pages) is kept between runs.

## 2. Reading the screen

- **Red bar (top-left)** — Consecration, your life. No natural regeneration.
- **Blue sliver (very top)** — Grace: fill it to level up and draft a new relic power.
- **Clock (top-centre)** — run time and the current **Bell**. Each bell toll (every 4
  minutes) wakes a deeper order of demon and sends a **Herald** hunting you.
- **Seven wisps (top-right)** — Vestiges claimed. Dim = still out there.
- **Gold chevron near your character** — the *Thread of the Rosary*, pointing to the
  nearest unclaimed Vestige (and to the Gate once it opens). Follow it.
- **Red screen edges** — you're below 35% Consecration. Disengage, find Bread.

## 3. Grace, levels, and the draft

Every slain demon releases a **grace ember** — walk near to collect (the Candle passive
widens this radius). When the blue bar fills, choose one of three cards:

- **New relic** (weapon) — up to **6** carried.
- **Relic level-up** — each weapon has 8 levels; damage, area, projectiles improve.
- **Passive relic** — up to 6, stat boosts (listed below).
- **Skip** ("Let them sleep") — small grace refund if nothing appeals.

Draft strategy 101: take a second weapon early (the Thurible alone won't hold a swarm),
prioritise levels in one or two damage relics over spreading thin, and grab **Deacon's
Stole** (cooldown) and **Bishop's Ring** (area) once your core weapons are set.

## 4. The Arsenal

| Relic | Behaviour | Best against |
|---|---|---|
| **The Thurible** *(start)* | Swings an arc before you, leaves burning incense | Frontal packs |
| **Holy Water** | Lobbed vials leave burning pools | Chokepoints, chasing crowds |
| **The Rosary** | Beads orbit you, striking on contact | Anything that gets close |
| **Psalter of War** | Homing verses seek the nearest demons | Fast single targets, Furies |
| **Communion Paten** | Golden disc pierces out and returns | Long lines of enemies |
| **Sanctus Bell** | Radial shockwave, **staggers** everything in radius | Emergency breathing room |
| **Pillar of the Choir** | Columns of light fall on random demons | Dense hordes at range |
| **Crown of Thorns** | Damage aura + retaliation when struck | Melee swarm pressure |
| **Sword of St. Michael** | Rare. Huge 360° sweep of white flame | Everything. Take it. |

### Exaltation

Raise a weapon to **level 8** and its **paired passive to rank 3**, and the next drafts
offer its **Exaltation** — a transfigured, far stronger form (the orbiting Censer of the
Seraphim, the twin-ring Sorrowful Mysteries, the never-sheathed Prince of the Host…).

| Weapon | Paired passive |
|---|---|
| Thurible | Embered Coal |
| Holy Water · Paten | Sacramental Wine |
| Rosary | Bishop's Ring |
| Psalter | Illuminated Manuscript |
| Sanctus Bell | Deacon's Stole |
| Pillar | Candle of the Vigil |
| Crown | Martyr's Palm |
| Sword | St. Christopher Medal |

### Passives

**Embered Coal** +Might · **Sacramental Wine** +Consecration · **Deacon's Stole**
−cooldowns · **St. Christopher Medal** +speed · **Candle of the Vigil** +gather radius ·
**Illuminated Manuscript** +Grace gained · **Bishop's Ring** +area ·
**Martyr's Palm** rise once from death.

## 5. Ground pickups

Smash the **bone piles** scattered everywhere (any weapon hit breaks them):

- **Bread of the Pilgrim** — heal 30.
- **Vial of Chrism** — every grace ember on the map flies to you.
- **Ossuary Mark token** — +3 meta-currency.
- **Wrath of the Lamb** *(radiant cross — rare)* — **step onto it deliberately**: purges
  every demon on and around the screen in a white flash. Save it for a Bell toll or a
  shrine fight if you can.

## 6. Know your enemy

| Demon | Threat | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Ash-imps | Slow swarm | The ocean you swim in |
| Gnashers | Fast lunge packs | Don't kite in straight lines |
| Bloatgrubs | Tanky, **acid pool on death** | Kill them at range, don't stand in green |
| Wailers | Drift in wobbling paths, phase past your knockback | Watch your flanks |
| Chain-brutes | Very tanky, knockback-resistant | Focus fire or walk away |
| Furies | Dive across the screen in bursts | The Psalter tracks them |
| Pyre-wights | **Immune to Thurible fire** | Holy damage only |
| Ledger-scribes | **Steal your grace embers** and flee | Kill on sight; they drop double |
| **Heralds** | Elite hunter at every bell toll | Killing one opens a bonus reliquary draft |
| **Shrinekeepers** | Named guardians of the seven shrines | Each fights differently: chargers, spitters, summoners |
| **The Warden** | The final battle at 18:00 | See below |

## 7. The shape of a run

- **Bells I–II (0:00–8:00)** — build your arsenal. Claim 2–3 nearby Vestiges while the
  keepers are still weak — they scale with the bells, so early shrine runs are the
  aggressive play.
- **Bell III–IV (8:00–16:00)** — the horde thickens; fire rivers make lanes dangerous
  (cross at the bone bridges; standing in lava burns *you*). Finish the shrine circuit.
- **Bell V (16:00–18:00)** — max pressure. Bank a Wrath of the Lamb if you can.
- **18:00 — THE WARDEN RISES.** Ordinary spawns stop. It telegraphs everything: dodge
  the sweeping chain (red line), sidestep the breath lanes, walk out of the fire cage
  before it closes. **Wounding it sheds grace embers** — the fight feeds you. Kill it,
  or survive to 21:00.
- **With 7/7 Vestiges** the Gate of Dawn opens 90 seconds into the fight — you don't
  have to kill the Warden; running for the door while it rages is a legitimate finish.

## 8. Between runs — the Chapel

**Ossuary Marks** (from elites, shrines, scribes, the Warden, and Mark tokens) persist
and buy permanent **Blessings** at the Reliquary Wall: Fervor (+Might), Vigor
(+Consecration), Alacrity (+speed), Providence (+draft rerolls). The **Vestige Book**
records every fragment of Lucia you have ever recovered, across all runs.

*"Ledgers, as Malacoda knows, can be amended."* — die freely; every descent leaves you stronger.
